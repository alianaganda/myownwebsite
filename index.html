    About Me

    Aliana Thea M. Alvarez is my name, you can call me Aliana or Yana.
    I will turn 18 on June 27, 2024. I am currently 17 years old. I reside in
    Bria Homes in San Gregorio. I enjoy eating, dancing, singing, and playing instruments,
    to name a few of my passions. I've competed in acting, dancing, and singing
    since elementary school, and I embrace failure as a necessary part of the process.
    I want to have a clear mindset, but sometimes I think "What I want is what I get."
    My goal is to make my two devoted parents and my younger sister proud of me.
    Since my mother led the dance ministry and I was the youngest member when I
    started, I was used to the church from a young age, especially in the area
    of dancing. I used to enjoy modeling as well, butswitched my attention to singing
    since it's my true talent and my mother encouraged me to follow it.
